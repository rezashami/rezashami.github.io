About


This is Reza Shami Tanha

Reza has studied Computer Science at the Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic Sciences(IASBS), Zanjan, Iran. He received his MSc in Artificial Intelligence in 2023 under the supervision of Dr. Mohsen Hooshmand and Dr. Mohsen Afsharchi. Furthermore, he got his BSc in Information Technology Engineering at IASBS in 2019.

His interest in understanding intelligent thinking by humans has led him to research how computers learn and reason. He tries to equip the computers to help humans.

His research interests focus on machine learning and its case studies, specifically graph neural networks (GNNs) and multi-agent reinforcement learning (MARL). His master's thesis is "Firefighting by an Autonomous UAVs Network with the Help of Deep Reinforcement Learning". The results of his research were published recently.

Moreover, he has learned programming during his studies. Because programming knowledge was necessary for his research, he started programming with Python and could acquire skills.

Reza is highly motivated to pursue a Ph.D. in computer science. His passion lies in advancing the field and pushing the boundaries of knowledge. With a focus on machine learning, he aims to explore new frontiers in artificial intelligence. Reza seeks to expand his expertise, conduct groundbreaking research, and make impactful contributions. His ultimate goal is to address real-world challenges and improve lives through technology.
